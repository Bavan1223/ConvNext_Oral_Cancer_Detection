### AI-Assisted Diagnosis using ConvNeXt & PyTorch

Welcome to **Next-Gen Oral Cancer Detection**, a deep learning project designed to assist in the early screening of oral lesions. This tool leverages **ConvNeXt**, a state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Network, to classify images as either **Cancerous** or **Non-Cancerous** with high precision.

Our goal is to provide a fast, non-invasive "second opinion" tool that bridges the gap between advanced AI research and practical medical application.
