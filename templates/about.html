About the Project

Mission

Our mission is to leverage artificial intelligence to assist in the early detection of oral cancer, making screening more accessible and helping save lives through timely intervention.

Technology

This application uses a state-of-the-art Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) based on the ConvNeXt architecture, trained on a diverse dataset of oral cavity images. The model has been optimized to distinguish between normal and potentially cancerous tissue with high accuracy.

Model Architecture:

ConvNeXt (Tiny variant)

Framework:

PyTorch with Flask backend

Input:

224x224 RGB images

Classes:

Binary classification (Normal/Cancerous)

About Oral Cancer

Oral cancer is a serious health condition that affects thousands of people worldwide. Key facts about oral cancer:

  • Early detection significantly improves survival rates (up to 80-90% 5-year survival for early-stage detection)
  • Common risk factors include tobacco use, alcohol consumption, and HPV infection
  • Regular screening and self-examination are crucial for early detection
  • Symptoms may include persistent mouth sores, lumps, or white/red patches

Limitations

While our AI model is designed to assist in screening, it has important limitations:

  • This tool is not a replacement for professional medical diagnosis
  • Image quality and lighting conditions can affect accuracy
  • The model may not detect all types of oral lesions or early-stage cancers
  • False positives and false negatives are possible
  • Always consult healthcare professionals for proper evaluation

How It Works

  1. Image Upload: User uploads an oral cavity image
  2. Preprocessing: Image is resized and normalized
  3. Analysis: CNN model processes the image through multiple layers
  4. Classification: Model predicts normal or cancerous tissue
  5. Results: Prediction and confidence score are displayed
